Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this really free forever?
Yes. It's open source under MIT License. No cost, ever. The core features will always remain free.
Q: Does it work offline?
Completely. No server upload required. Everything processes locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and speed.
Q: Is my data private?
Totally. No analytics, no telemetry, no cloud by default. Your files never leave your computer unless you explicitly choose to sync with Pro features.
Q: What platforms are supported?
PDFGround works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Built with Tauri for native performance and small file size.
Q: Can I contribute to the project?
Absolutely! PDFGround is open source. Check out our GitHub repository to contribute code, report bugs, or suggest features.
Q: What's the difference between Free and Pro?
The free version includes all core PDF tools and works completely offline. Pro ($2.99/month) adds cloud sync, AI features, and unlimited batch processing to support ongoing development.
